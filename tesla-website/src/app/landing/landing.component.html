Key Features

Zero-emission electric vehicles powered by advanced electric drivetrains.

Cutting-edge Autopilot features for semi-autonomous driving.

Impressive driving range on a single charge, providing convenience for long trips.

Performance

Tesla's flagship performance model, the Model S Plaid, is known for its astonishing acceleration, with 0 to 60 mph times under 2 seconds.

Electric motors provide immediate and consistent torque from a standstill, resulting in rapid acceleration and responsiveness.

Many Tesla models come standard with dual-motor AWD, providing improved traction and stability in various driving conditions, including wet and snowy roads.

Sustainability

Tesla's battery technology is designed to have a long lifespan, which reduces the need for frequent battery replacements and waste.

Tesla offers solar energy products, including solar panels and solar roofs, which enable homes and businesses to generate clean energy from sunlight.
